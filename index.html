Women Startup Summit

KSUM plan to organize a Women Startup Summit on 1st August 2019 with the Support of Indian Women Network, a network of women promoted by Confederation of Indian Industries (CII) With a view to encourage the women entrepreneurs. The summit is envisaged as an attempt to encourage aspiring women professionals to take up the entrepreneurial journey.
